About us

eMobilis Technology Institute, based in Nairobi, Kenya, is a premier, TVET-accredited coding and technology training institution. Founded in 2008, it focuses on upskilling African youth in software development, data science, cybersecurity, and cloud computing to meet the continent's growing ICT demands.

They offer highly flexible class scheduling to accommodate students and working professionals, including morning, afternoon, evening, and Saturday classes. They also allow students to pay in installments and feature monthly course intakes.

Our mission

The organization focuses on responsible modernization, ensuring that automation and technology strengthen governance and empower teams without compromise.
